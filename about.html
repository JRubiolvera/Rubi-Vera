Un poco sobre mí...

I still remember the day when, upon handing in an art project, the teacher asked me if I was going to study Fine Arts, almost as if he wanted me to confirm it. Perhaps he thought I had talent for it. However, I replied, "No, sir, I'm going to study Electronics." I have always enjoyed everything related to visual arts, and I cannot deny that I will always carry that thorn. Nevertheless, studying electronics has provided me with a foundation to delve into the world of computer science since ancient times, when operating systems were simply a string of commands.

Regardless, if we were to trace each of my options back then, Fine Arts and Electronics, like two continuous lines, we would reach a point where destiny would intersect them. It is precisely at that point where I find myself now: pursuing this training cycle that, one could say, bridges these two worlds. Furthermore, currently, I am tinkering in the fascinating world of artificial intelligence, especially exploring image and animation generation, specifically with the Stable Diffusion language model.
